Ortigas Center

25 ADB Avenue, Ortigas Center, Pasig City, Metro Manila

Ortigas Center is a central business district located within the joint boundaries of Pasig, Mandaluyong and Quezon City, within the Metro Manila region in the Philippines.

From Palengke to Megamall (Ride UV Megamall - Php 30)

The People

In Ortigas Center, you'll find both wealthy residents and office workers. This busy district hosts upscale businesses and luxurious living spaces, attracting affluent individuals and white-collar professionals alike.

Food

If you're in Ortigas Center and craving a quick and satisfying meal, you can't go wrong with the classic choices of McDonald's and Jollibee. McDonald's offers familiar favorites like their iconic Big Mac and crispy Chicken McNuggets, while Jollibee serves up Filipino comfort food staples like Chickenjoy and Jolly Spaghetti. Whether you're in the mood for a juicy burger or crave the taste of homegrown flavors, both fast food chains in Ortigas Center have something to satisfy every appetite.
